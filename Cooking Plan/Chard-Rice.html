Chard Rice

How to Cook It

4 Portions
Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown rice
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 bunch rainbow chard
  • 3 Tbsp. Olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  1. Place the brown rice in a pot with 1 Tbsp of olive, 1 tsp salt, oil and 3 cups of water
  2. Place on medium heat until it begins to boil, then turn the heat to low and put a lid on. Cook for 20 minutes. After 20 minutes, mix you rice.If there is still water, continue cooking, if not taste to make sure it is done and turn off the heat. You can also use a rice cooker if you have one.
  3. Wash the chard. Tear the leafy part from the stalks. Cut off the base of the chard stock, then dice it. Roughly chop the chard leaves.
  4. Put a pan on the stove on medium heat and add then remaining 2 Tbsp of olive oil. Add the chard stocks and cook for 3-4 minutes until slightly soft.
  5. Add the leaves and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the rice and mix together and continue to cook. Sprinkle about 1tsp of salt and pepper each and then taste. Continue to season to taste. When satisfied, serve.
