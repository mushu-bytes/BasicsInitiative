Vegetarian Tofu Tacos

How to Cook It

Serves 3-6
Ingredients:

  • 14 oz of firm tofu (average sized tofu package)
  • 2 cups of shredded red cabbage
  • ½ cup cilantro
  • 1 bunch scallions
  • 1 ½ Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 limes
  • ¼ yogurt
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 ½ Tbsp of taco seasoning
  • 6, 8-inch tortillas (flour or corn)
  • ¼ cup shredded mozzarella or pepper jack
  • ¼ cup salsa
  1. Drain the tofu and cut into 8 slices
  2. Lay the tofu slices flat on a stack of paper towel and then put another layer on top. Put a heavy skillet on top and let sit for 10 minutes to press out any extra water.
  3. Shred the cabbage and slice the scallions. Also, zest one of the limes and juice both Then toss together the cilantro, cabbage, scallions with the lime zest, half the lime juice, and 1 Tbsp olive oil.
  4. Mix the yogurt with the other half of the lime juice and add salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Brush the tofu with the remaining ½ Tbsp of olive oil and sprinkle with the taco seasoning. Cook in a nonstick skillet for five minutes then flip and cook for another two minutes. Take them out of the pan and cut into strips.
  6. Toast the tortillas in a dry skillet for one minute on each side then fill with the prepared ingredients
