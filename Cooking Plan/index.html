Meet the Team

Adam Wadhwani

My name is Adam Wadhwani and I am a 17 year-old high school student from Sacramento, California. I have spent the majority of my life with an enthusiasm for being in the kitchen. I have cooked as part of private events, national competitions, fundraisers, community benefit projects, and as an instructor for the past four years at the Sacramento Natural Foods Coop cooking school. More than anything, I love sharing my knowledge of cooking with others and can’t wait to do it here.

Damon Lin

My name is Damon Lin and I am also a 17 year-old high school student from Sacramento, California. Like some of the kids my age, I have very little knowledge or experience cooking sustainable, healthy, and affordable meals. Going off to college and moving out will definitely challenge me to take better care of myself, and this project will certainly provide me with the tools to do so.

Dana Andrak

One of the first things I noticed when I moved to Sacramento was the abundance of beautiful fruits and vegetables all year long. It didn’t take long for me to realize, how much good food was being thrown away and how many people were hungry. For me food is love, self love, and love for others and an essential for keeping us healthy both physically and mentally. Nutritious food is within everyones reach, especially in Sacramento! Teaching people about label reading, how to shop on a budget, and cooking healthy meals is an especially gratifying way to connect with and give back to my community.
